About the Founder & Creator

Shaikh Ziadul Haque is a versatile creative professional, author, playwright, 3D artist, and digital entrepreneur. Driven by an innovative perspective and a deep sense of social responsibility, he merges artistic storytelling with modern digital craftsmanship through NRS Creation.

🎭 Literary & Theatre

Active member of Aranyak Natyadal. Author of We Are Responsible and the Inspirational book series.

Skilled in Blender. Founder of NRS Creation producing abstract wall art and VFX.
